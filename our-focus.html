    Our Focus

    Mission Behavioral Health is committed to providing quality, patient-focused care to seniors with primary psychiatric diagnoses.

    Our committed and compassionate staff provides complete, personalized care tailored to each patient’s psychological and physical needs, aiming for the very best patient outcomes.

    Recognizing the critical role of environment in patient progress, our inpatient hospital is intentionally designed to create a safe and pleasant atmosphere conducive to effective and successful treatment.

    Each patient receives a tailored treatment plan put together specifically for the individual by our multidisciplinary team.

    We specialize in treating conditions such as:

    • Anxiety
    • Psychosis
    • Schizophrenia
    • Behavior disturbances
    • Depression
    • Alzheimer’s disease and other forms of dementia
    • Mental disorders resulting from brain injury, stroke, or other neuropsychiatric conditions

    Assessments

    Our referral process starts with a single call to the clinical team. Upon receiving your call, our clinical team will:

    • Conduct initial phone screening
    • Handle paperwork/documentation copying or faxing
    • Make necessary phone calls to family and/or powers of attorney
    • Arrange transportation
    • Provide post-discharge follow-ups
